God's Love

God loves us so much that he sent his only son down to Earth to die for us. It's an important fact that is often taken for granted. Sometimes we may want to rebel agaisnt God because life isn't going our way, but it is important to remember his love for us. He wants what is best for us even if it is not what we are hoping for. He blesses us this way.

Scriptures about his love for us:

  • Deuteronomy 7:8
  • Psalms 31:23
  • John 3:16

Second Coming

The second coming is an interesting and mysterious topic. Endless generations have tried to predict when it will happen. Others have tried to predict how it will happen. No one knows for sure the details of the second coming. What we do know is that we should prepare ourselves for when it does happen. By making ourselves worthy and devoting our lives to God, we will not be caught off guard. Some think that they can do what they want and then repent at the last second. Unfortunately, since we don't know when the time will be, this would not be a wise choice. Being prepared to be reunited with our Heavenly Father is the only way to be sure he will accept us into his kingdom.

Scriptures about the second coming:

  • Matthew 16:27
  • Revelation 1:7
  • Matthew 25:1-13

Forgiveness

Forgiving others is not an easy thing to do. we often get caught up on the actions of others that we forget about the bigger picture. Holding grudges towards those who have hurt us is not only useless, but it can stop us from moving on and growing as children of God. The second commandment says, "Thou shall love thy neighbor". I believe a huge part of showing love for our neighbors includes offering forgiveness. God forgives us when we do him wrong so it is best to follow his example forgive as well.

Scriptures about forgiveness:

  • Luke 17:3
  • D&C 82:1
  • Matthew 6:15
